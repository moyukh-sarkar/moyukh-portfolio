About

Let's dive into my story.

I am Moyukh Sarkar, an ardent devotee of dance. To be specific I am a choreographer, performer and a trainer specialized in versatile dance forms such as Classical dance (Odissi), Rabindranritya, folk ( Baul, Dhamail, Kirtan, Bhatiali, Dhunuchi dance) and Contemporary.

I embarked on the divine journey as a dancer for more than twenty years being a disciple of Guru Proma Abonti. My projects and choreographies bear the essence of the traditional roots of Bangladeshi culture draped in the forms of expression in timeless contemporary movements.

At present I am serving as an enlisted artist of Bangladesh Television, Dance Trainer at 'Fatikchhari Shilpokola Academy', 'Fatikchhari Shishu Academy' and 'Odissi and Tagore Dance Movement Centre, Chittagong'. Along with this I have received the honour to represent the culture of my country, Bangladesh in national and international scales.

Expertise

My key areas of expertise.

I am focusing on an individualistic approach to my contemporary choreographies that will stimulate the divine energy in my spectators I obtain while I dance. While I stick to the rigid and traditional roots of Odissi and Rabindranritya to uphold the essence of my culture and the quintessence of sancity.

Odissi

I have been practicing one of the most graceful Indian Classical Dances, Odissi for the last twenty years under the surveillance of my Guru Proma Abonti. I have also been fortunate to attend workshops of Guru Poushali Mukherjee and Guru Kiran Segal. As a recognition to my journey, I had been qualified as one of the top five Odissi dancers of Bangladesh by the Association of Dancers, Bangladesh (Nrittoshilpi Shongstha, Bangladesh), the best dancer in national level (Odissi) and been honoured with the international award, 'Odissi Prativa award, 2023'. I have also taken part as an Assistant Choreographer of my Guru Proma Abonti in various performances of Odissi and Tagore Dance Movement Centre. I believe that Odissi is a medium of worship to me.

Contemporary

In my odyssey with contemporary dance, I eventually realised that this dance form is the expression of one's soul. I have been fortunate to perform in various productions of Odissi and Tagore Dance Movement Centre which were based on contemporary dance. I have gained the privilege of attending workshops my eminent Contemporary performer and choreographer Kossi Sebastian Aholo-Wokawui from Germany. As an acknowledgement of my individualistic approach to contemporary I have been awarded with the first position in talent show (Uchhash Lions International Leo Youth Camp, 2022 and Somriddhir Pothe Lions International Leo Youth Camp, 2024) and have been selected as one of the top ten dancers by the Association of Dancers, Bangladesh (Nrittoshilpi Shongstha, Bangladesh). I am in an endeavour to blend the folk dance styles of Bangladesh Baul, Dhamail, Kirtan, Bhatiali, Dhunuchi dance in my contemporary choreographies.

Rabindranritya

As of any other Bengali, Tagore's philosophy can coincide with every manifestation of my inventive self. I have devoted myself in the practice of the pure traditional shantiniketan dance style of tagore under the supervision of my Guru Proma Abonti. I have also been blessed with the opportunity to take part in workshops by eminent Tagore philosophers from India, Amartya Mukhapadhay sir, Sruti Badopadhyay ma'am and Sharmila Banerjee ma'am from Bangladesh.

Miscellaneous

I have also received a four year training from Zila Shilpokola Academy, Chittagong on dance styles such as Kathhak, Bharatnatyam, Monipuri, Contemporary and Folk. Along with this I have taken part in a three days workshop by eminent Monipuri dancer, Warda Rehab from Bangladesh. I am also a theatre worker under the group 'Kothashundor' guided by Professor Dr Kuntal Barua. I have also been a part of the 'Institute of Mime and Movement' and performed in different international events on behalf of the institute. Additionally I am working as a professional model and actress. I have been fortunate to play main roles in two web series, one TVC and three shortfilms.
